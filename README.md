## üîç Deep Dive Insights

### 1. Profitability & Seasonality
* **Peak Months:** January, March, August, and October show the highest profitability.
* **Loss Identification:** The business experienced a notable **net loss in July** and a minor dip in **December**.
* **High-Margin Products:** **Printers** and **Bookcases** are the top profit-contributing sub-categories.

### 2. Product Category Distribution
The inventory is divided into three main segments:
* **Clothing (62.62%):** The primary driver of sales volume.
* **Electronics (20.55%):** Moderate volume but typically higher price points.
* **Furniture (16.83%):** The smallest segment by quantity.

### 3. Payment Behavior
Understanding how customers pay is crucial for logistics and cash flow:
* **Cash on Delivery (44%):** Remains the most popular method, indicating a high reliance on physical cash transactions.
* **UPI (21%):** The leading digital payment method.
* **Cards/EMI (35%):** Combined share of Credit Cards, Debit Cards, and EMI options.

### 4. Geographical Performance
* **Top State:** **Maharashtra** is the clear leader in sales, followed by Madhya Pradesh and Uttar Pradesh.
* **Customer Loyalty:** Top customers like **Harivansh** and **Madhav** contribute significantly to the total revenue.
